                  Disclaimer

                  All technology encodes ways of seeing and this tool is no exception.

                  As such, all acts of data collection and visualisation are inherently reductive and subjective. The stories behind every decision and the dynamic of every meeting cannot be known except to those who were there.

                  While using this tool, never forget the human being it means to represent and thus use this tool as a starting point to reach out to the councillors and officers it mentions instead of a means to avoid them.

                  Take heed and be warned.
